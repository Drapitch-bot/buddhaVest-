# BuddhaVest — Google Play Store Listing

> Wording aligned with the app's own disclaimer: BuddhaVest is a **research tool**, not a
> financial advisor. The score is a **transparent, fixed-formula calculation**, not AI and
> not a buy/sell recommendation. No "AI advisor" / "recommendation" / "advice" language —

**Short Description** (max 80 chars)


**Full Description** (max 4000 chars)
```
BuddhaVest is a personal research tool that organizes public financial information clearly — so you can think for yourself before you decide. It is not a financial advisor and does not tell you what to buy or sell.

🧘 ONE TRANSPARENT CONCLUSION
Each stock gets a single score from a fixed, transparent formula — 40% business quality, 35% valuation, 25% cash flow & dividends. The exact same math for every company. It's a research signal, not a buy or sell recommendation.

📊 THE FULL PICTURE
Behind every score: financial health, valuation metrics, earnings history, upcoming events, and market news — all from public data.

🌍 4 LANGUAGES
Fully available in English, Hebrew, Russian, and Spanish, with on-the-fly translation of market news.

⚡ REAL-TIME DATA
Live prices, market movers, the VIX volatility index, and financial news — updated continuously.

📋 WATCHLIST & JOURNAL
Save tickers and keep private notes — stored only on your device.

📈 PRICE CHARTS
Interactive price history for 1W, 1M, 3M, 6M, 1Y, and 5Y.

💡 PLAIN-LANGUAGE METRICS
Every metric includes a simple explanation so you learn as you research.

BuddhaVest is free. It is not a licensed advisor, does not guarantee returns, and is not a substitute for professional financial advice. Always do your own research. Market data from Yahoo Finance, Tiingo, Google News, and Stooq.
```
