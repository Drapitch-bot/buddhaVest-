```

**Full Description** (max 4000 chars)
```
BuddhaVest gives you clear, AI-powered stock recommendations — Buy, Hold, or Avoid — so you always know where you stand before making a move.

🧘 SIMPLE DECISIONS
Every stock gets a single verdict with a confidence score. No noise, no jargon — just a clear signal backed by data.

Live stock prices, market movers, VIX volatility index, and breaking financial news — all updated continuously.

📋 WATCHLIST
Save your favorite tickers and track them at a glance from your personal watchlist.

📈 PRICE CHARTS
Interactive price history with touch-to-explore tooltips for 1W, 1M, 3M, 6M, 1Y, and 5Y periods.

💡 METRIC EXPLANATIONS
Every financial metric includes a plain-language explanation so you can learn as you invest.

BuddhaVest is free to use. Market data is provided by Tiingo and Yahoo Finance.
